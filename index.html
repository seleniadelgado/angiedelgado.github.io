Hello there, my name is Angie. I’m from La Fortuna, Costa Rica and I am a front end software engineer. I moved to San Francisco where I attended San Francisco State University. I earned my Bachelor’s of Arts degree in linguistics which has given me the opportunity and desire to learn and understand different people, their languages, and their cultures. This type of empathy has really shaped the approach I take when thinking of web development.

I recently completed my studies at Holberton School of Software Engineering. The curriculum focuses on Javascript, HTML/CSS, Python, C, devops principles, networking and databases as well as working in teams and peer learning throughout the projects. I now love all things Javascript, CSS and HTML. When I learned about accessibility and UX, something clicked within me and I instantly realized that this is the path I want for my career. Anytime I am able to help someone with technology, I cannot help but wonder is there a way this could be easier for them?” Front end software engineering has allowed me to express myself and motivates me to make other people’s lives easier.

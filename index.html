Angie Delgado

Hi there, I’m Angie, a UX designer out of San Francisco, California. For the past year, I have been dedicating myself to be the best UX designer I can be. I approach design with an empathetic outlook, keeping the user’s experience in mind each step of the way. I truly believe the best way to solve a problem is to break it down into smaller pieces and understand it in and out. My background in software engineering and my interest in people have become the perfect recipe for a career in UX. If you are still reading this, some fun facts about me: I was born in Costa Rica and raised at the footsteps of an active volcano. I once danced tango in front of a large audience in Buenos Aires, and I have mailed a postcard from the southernmost postal office in the world. If you haven’t guessed, when I’m not designing I am travelling, cooking or hanging out with my cat.
